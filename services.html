Our Services

Full-Time or Part-Time Students

Drop your pup off at school! This new service gives us the opportunity to give our communitya safe, clean & trustworthy place to send their dogs for a full day of structured socialization, obedience & enrichment. Our new facility offers many new amenities such as multiple socialization areas, all levels of distraction for proofing your pup's skils, a stream & pond to adventure through, 4 acres of trails to hike & much more!

Each pup will relax in their own kennel suite to enjoy any down time, snack/lunch time & water breaks. This service allows for customization!

This service requires owners to pick up & drop off their pups at our facility.

Drop Off Times: 7 - 9am

Pick Up Times: 4 - 6pm
