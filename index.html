Personal Paws

Serving the Capital Region in Upstate NY, located in Rotterdam, NY

Our goal is to help educate our community on canines and their needs. With our extensive education and experiences, we want to not only pay it forward by helping each dog become the best versions of themselves, but by also helping create healthy, well rounded relationships between canines and their humans.

Meet The Crew

Liz and Kait together have 8 years of combined experience in the canine industry, from volunteering to helping in local shelters; kennel work in daycare and fitness programs to managing facilities and employees. To handling canine behaviors, basic obedience, competitive handling skill’s, reading body language of canines, and extensive pet care. All of this has led the two of them where they are today! Liz and Kait take pride in their customer service skills along with their ability to change the quality of relationship between canine and human. They have devoted 100% of their lives to helping dogs in need and have so much to offer their community and surrounding communities.
